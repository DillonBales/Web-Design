This final list describes 7 different types of governments

Democracy
Power is vested in the people
Communism
Aims to not have classes and wealth is shared equally
Monarchy
The government is run by a single person for life, and is passed down the bloodline
Republic
Political power lies with the people through representatives.
Totalitarianism
Opposing political parties are prohibited and society is controlled by the government
Federalism
A government that divides the power between the federal government and local authorities
Theocracy
A government where one or more deities are recognized as the supreme ruler(s)
